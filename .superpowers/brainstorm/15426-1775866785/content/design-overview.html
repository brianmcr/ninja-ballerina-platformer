Design: Game Overview & Structure

Section 1 of 5 — confirm before we move on

Concept

Ninja Ballerina — a 2D side-scrolling platformer where a pigtailed ballerina fights through a dojo/dance studio hybrid overrun by Soggy Waffle and his breakfast goons. Aimed at ages 8-12, family-friendly, forgiving difficulty.

Tech Stack

  • Engine: Kaplay (TypeScript)
  • Build: Vite
  • Art: Pixel art sprite sheets (generated/placeholder to start)
  • Level design: Tiled map editor → JSON import
  • Audio: Kaplay built-in audio (add later)

World 1: Dojo Studio

6 levels + 1 boss fight against Soggy Waffle

  • Levels 1-2: Tutorial — running, jumping, spin attack. Balance beams, hanging silks as platforms.
  • Levels 3-4: Introduce enemies — butter packet goons, gluten blobs. Ribbon whip, cartwheel dash needed.
  • Level 5: Ninja powerup introduced — wall-jump sections, shuriken targets unlock new paths.
  • Level 6: Gauntlet combining all mechanics.
  • Boss: Soggy Waffle — headband-wearing waffle sensei. Multi-phase fight in the main dojo hall.
