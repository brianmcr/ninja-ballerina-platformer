Design: Level Design & Environment

Section 3 of 5 — confirm before we move on

Setting: The Dojo Studio

A hybrid ninja dojo / ballet studio. Wooden floors, mirrored walls, hanging silks, balance beams, punching bags, bamboo screens, paper lanterns. The deeper you go, the more Soggy Waffle has corrupted it — syrup stains, butter smears, sticky gluten webs.

Platform Types

Balance Beams

Standard solid platforms. Narrow ones require precision.

Hanging Silks

Swinging platforms. Move back and forth, time your jump.

Bamboo Scaffolding

One-way platforms — jump up through, stand on top.

Punching Bags

Bounce pads — land on them to launch high.

Mirrored Walls

Wall-cling/wall-jump surfaces (ninja form only).

Syrup Floors

Hazard — slows movement. Cartwheel dash to cross quickly.

Level Breakdown

Level 1 — Welcome to the Studio

Teaches: Run, jump, pirouette spin, ribbon whip

Setting: Clean, bright studio. Simple gaps and low platforms.

Soggy Waffle: Opening cutscene — crashes through the wall, steals the golden ballet slippers, taunts and escapes.

Enemies: Butter Pats only. Ninja powerup in a visible alcove.

Level 2 — Silk & Shadows

Teaches: Graceful float, hanging silk timing

Setting: Vertical section with silks. Starting to see syrup stains.

Soggy Waffle: Visible in background on a high platform, laughing. Sends a wave of Butter Pats, then escapes through a locked door.

Enemies: Butter Pats, introduce Gluten Blobs. Ninja powerup behind a wall-jump section.

Level 3 — The Training Grounds

Teaches: Cartwheel dash (through barriers and enemies)

Setting: Outdoor training yard behind the dojo. Bamboo scaffolding, punching bags.

Soggy Waffle: No direct appearance. His graffiti ("WAFFLE WUZ HERE") and syrup traps show his influence.

Enemies: All prior + Syrup Drippers. Ninja powerup on a high bamboo platform.

Level 4 — The Sticky Kitchen

Teaches: Syrup floors, combined movement (dash + float combos)

Setting: Dojo kitchen area. Countertops, pots, syrup everywhere.

Soggy Waffle: Mid-level chase sequence — he throws syrup bombs as you pursue him. Escapes at the end by flooding the room.

Enemies: All types. Introduce Milk Carton Guards. Ninja powerup in a side room.

Level 5 — Above the Rafters

Teaches: Mastery level — all mechanics in challenging combinations

Setting: High up in the dojo rafters. Long drops, tight wall-jump corridors, swinging silks over pits.

Soggy Waffle: No direct appearance. But his corruption is heavy — gluten webs blocking paths, syrup waterfalls.

Enemies: Dense enemy placement. Ninja powerup early (you'll need it).

Level 6 — The Gauntlet

Teaches: Final test before the boss

Setting: Long corridor leading to the main dojo hall. Traps, enemies, and timed sequences.

Soggy Waffle: Taunts from behind glass barriers. Activates traps remotely. You can see him getting nervous as you advance.

Enemies: All types, hardest configurations. Two ninja powerups available.

Boss — Soggy Waffle Showdown

Arena: Main dojo hall. Wide, flat with platforms at different heights.

Phase 1: Soggy Waffle throws syrup bombs in arcs. Dodge and hit him with shurikens or close-range attacks. 3 hits to advance.

Phase 2: He floods the floor with syrup, forcing you to wall-jump between platforms. Summons Butter Pat waves. Hit him when he pauses to taunt.

Phase 3: Enraged — faster attacks, throws Gluten Blobs that stick to walls. Final 3 hits to defeat. Golden ballet slippers recovered!

Collectibles

  • Sequins — coins equivalent. Scattered everywhere. 100 = extra life.
  • Ninja Powerup — glowing shuriken star. At least one per level.
  • Hidden Ribbons — 3 per level, hidden in hard-to-reach spots. Completionist reward.
