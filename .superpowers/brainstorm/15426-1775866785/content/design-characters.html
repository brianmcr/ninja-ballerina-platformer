Design: Characters & Mechanics

Section 2 of 5 — confirm before we move on

Player Character: Ninja Ballerina

Base Form: Ballerina

Caucasian girl, black pigtails, pink/white leotard and tutu

  • Run / Jump — standard platformer movement
  • Pirouette Spin — close-range attack, damages enemies on contact
  • Graceful Float — hold jump to descend slowly (like Peach)
  • Cartwheel Dash — quick horizontal burst, passes through enemies
  • Ribbon Whip — short-range directional attack

Powered Up: Ninja Ballerina

Outfit adds black ninja wrap over leotard, headband, arm wraps

  • All base moves retained
  • + Shuriken Throw — ranged projectile attack
  • + Wall-Cling — grab walls, slide slowly
  • + Wall-Jump — jump off walls to reach new areas

Soggy Waffle (Villain)

Appearance: Humanoid soggy waffle wearing a ninja headband. Syrup dripping, menacing but cartoonish. Moves with exaggerated martial arts poses.

Appearances Before Boss Fight

  • Level 1: Cutscene intro — Soggy Waffle crashes the studio, steals something precious (golden ballet slippers?), taunts and flees
  • Level 2: Appears in background, sends butter goons, escapes through a door you can't reach yet
  • Level 4: Brief mid-level encounter — throws syrup traps, you chase him but he escapes
  • Level 6: Taunts from behind barriers as you fight through his gauntlet
  • Boss: Full multi-phase fight in the main dojo hall

Enemies

Butter Pats

Basic patrol enemy. Slides back and forth. One hit to defeat. Leaves a slippery patch on death.

Gluten Blobs

Sticky enemies that cling to platforms. Jump on them and they stretch — takes two hits. Can trap player briefly.

Syrup Drippers

Ceiling-mounted. Drop syrup puddles that slow movement. Shuriken to destroy from range.

Milk Carton Guards

Taller enemies with a shield. Must cartwheel-dash behind them or hit with shuriken. Block frontal attacks.

Ninja Powerup Placement

Every level contains at least one ninja powerup pickup (glowing shuriken star). Some are in plain sight, some reward exploration. Wall-jump sections exist in every level — accessible only in ninja form, always optional but rewarding (bonus collectibles, shortcuts).
